# Outgrow
> *Growing Capital. Growing Futures.*

A closed-loop agri-capital platform connecting investors, smallholder farmers, and exporters across Africa.

---

## Overview

Outgrow removes the financial and logistical barriers that prevent smallholder farmers from participating in the global export economy. Investors get a transparent agricultural pipeline. Exporters get consistent, quality supply. Farmers get everything they need to grow — upfront, at no cost.

**The farmer's only job is to farm. Outgrow handles everything else.**

---

## The Problem

African agriculture is trapped in a three-sided deadlock:

- **Farmers** have fertile land but cannot access capital, quality inputs, or export markets
- **Investors** want agricultural exposure but have no trusted, transparent pipeline
- **Exporters** need consistent, high-quality supply but cannot depend on fragmented smallholders

---

## How It Works

### 1. Farmer Registration
The farmer submits their details via SMS or the Outgrow web portal. They select their country and choose from a curated list of export-grade crops available in their region.

### 2. Field Verification
A trained Outgrow field worker visits the farm to verify soil quality, water access, and the farmer's capability to grow the selected crop to the required standard.

### 3. Input Provisioning
Once approved, Outgrow supplies everything the farmer needs — seeds, fertiliser, IoT monitoring devices, and any other required inputs — at no upfront cost to the farmer.

### 4. AI-Powered Monitoring
Outgrow's team monitors each farm remotely using IoT sensor data and satellite imagery. An AI layer identifies issues early and communicates guidance to the farmer in their preferred language and channel — SMS, WhatsApp, or AI chat.

### 5. Harvest Collection
At harvest, Outgrow's logistics team collects the produce and retrieves the IoT devices. Devices are refurbished and redeployed to the next cycle.

### 6. Payment and Delivery
The farmer receives their agreed profit share. Produce is graded, packaged, and delivered to pre-registered exporters matched by Outgrow's platform.

---

## The AI Layer

Outgrow is a technology platform at its core. AI powers five critical functions:

- **Smart Onboarding** — NLP captures structured farmer data over SMS in local languages, removing the need for literacy or smartphone access
- **Predictive Monitoring** — IoT and satellite data feeds a model that detects crop stress, disease risk, and weather threats before they become losses
- **Risk Scoring** — each farm receives a live AI-generated risk and yield probability score, giving investors transparent, data-backed ROI projections
- **Multilingual Communication** — farmers receive actionable advice in Swahili, Luganda, Amharic, and other local languages through their channel of choice
- **Exporter Matching** — harvest quality, volume, and timing are automatically matched to the most suitable registered exporter

## The Platform

Two interfaces. Four stakeholders.

### Farmer Portal
Mobile-first and low-bandwidth friendly.

- Register via SMS or web with country and crop selection
- Track application status from submission through to active farm
- View input delivery updates and farm milestones
- Receive AI-powered farm advice in local language via preferred channel

### Admin Dashboard
A role-aware internal dashboard with four views:

| View | Who it serves | What it shows |
|---|---|---|
| Investor View | Investors | Portfolio, risk scores, yield projections, ROI |
| Field Worker View | Field workers | Visit queue, verification checklist, assessment submission |
| Exporter View | Exporters | Harvest-ready listings by crop, volume, grade, and location |
| Ops View | Internal team | Full pipeline, IoT alerts, logistics, communication logs |

## Business Model

- **Input Margin** — bulk procurement distributed to farmers at cost-plus
- **Export Commission** — percentage of each final export sale
- **Investor Management Fee** — flat fee or profit-sharing on capital deployed
- **Data Licensing** — anonymised farm data licensed to agri-insurers and commodity platforms

---

## Market Entry Strategy

1. Launch with two to three high-value export crops in a single country to prove the model
2. Partner with existing farmer cooperatives for the first cohort to reduce onboarding cost
3. Onboard two to three anchor exporters before the first harvest to guarantee offtake
4. Use first-season data to refine the AI risk model and expand to new crops and countries

---

## Why Outgrow Wins

- **Verified Farmer Network** — building trust and verification at farm level takes time and field presence; it cannot be replicated quickly
- **Closed-Loop Model** — Outgrow controls quality from soil to shipment, giving exporters and investors confidence
- **Reusable Infrastructure** — IoT devices returned after each harvest are refurbished and redeployed, improving unit economics over time
- **Deep Local Focus** — country-specific crop selection means genuine domain expertise rather than shallow global coverage
